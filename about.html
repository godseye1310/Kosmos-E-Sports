The KOSMOS E−Sports

We announced our entry to Valorant on Feb 12, 2021, by signing its first player, Mahakud "LinenRat" Sugyan on the Valorant roster. The team is currently managed by Avinash "GodsEye" Kumar. In March 2021, We formed our first ever esports team for Valorant, known as Team KOSMOS; our first original Valorant roster competes under the name KOSMOS S5.

About Team Kosmos

Team Kosmos is a multi-regional professional esports organization based in the INDIA that was founded in 2021. With the release of Valorant, Team Kosmos signed their first professional players. In 2021, Team Kosmos acquired a State Valorant team, marking our first venture into e-esports industries.

Inception

In January 2021, Team Kosmos officially merged with Team L-Rat under the Kosmos banner, bringing on Sugyan Mahakud, in our supporting staff, the former COD, Street Fighter, and Assassins Creed; L-Rat teams. Their COD squad won CWL Pro League 2019, which is a global professional Call of Duty offline league operated by Activision, MLG, and Treyarch. Team Kosmos S5 Valorant team has won four State titles, and our PUBG team was awarded PUBG Continental Series-5 Champions in 2020, a feat achieved in just four tournaments

Striving for Excellence

We’re working to build one of the most successful and recognizable Esports organization in the world. We try to approach everything we do thoughtfully and creatively — and if you feel the same about your work, we’d love to hear from you!

The Kosmos Culture

With offices in Hyderabad and remote employees both in the country and across the globe, we’re a passionate group of people from around the world.

Our collaborative, dynamic culture allows our teams to work cross-functionally and optimize productivity. We maintain a progressive outlook, always looking to the future and identifying trends to set ourselves apart from the competition and to be at the forefront of the world of Esports.

Founders

Avinash "GodsEye" Kumar is a founding member, and currently co-owner and chief executive officer of Team Kosmos. He originally competed in PUBG Continentel Series prior to forming Team Kosmos.

Mahakud "LinenRat" Sugyan joined the Team Kosmos staff when Team L-Rat merged with Team Kosmos. Since the merge, Sugyan has taken the role of co-owner and chief President and primarily manages the Valorant E-Sports Team Kosmos S5.
